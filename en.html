Our Story

Vietnamese Cooking Class in Da Nang

Welcome to Family Kitchen Da Nang, a truly unique experience where you don't just take a cooking class, you become part of our family. We open the doors of our own home and cook together, just like we do every day.

We are a Franco-Vietnamese couple, both former professional chefs, passionate about sharing the flavors and traditions of Vietnamese cuisine. Our classes are intimate, warm, and filled with laughter, the way a family meal should be.

Programme

What to Expect in Your Cooking Class

A journey through Vietnamese flavours, from market to table 🌿

1

☕ Vietnamese Egg Coffee

Upon arrival, discover this unique Vietnamese specialty: a creamy, sweet coffee made with whipped eggs. A truly unforgettable treat to start the experience.

2

🛒 Local Market Tour

Off to the neighbourhood market! Together we select fresh herbs, vegetables and the day's ingredients, an authentic immersion into everyday Vietnamese life.

3

🥗 Vietnamese Salad & Fresh Spring Rolls

Back home, we prepare a fresh and colourful salad alongside homemade spring rolls, light, healthy and absolutely delicious.

4

🥞 Bánh Xèo, Crispy Vietnamese Rice Pancakes

Master the art of Bánh Xèo, golden, crispy rice flour pancakes filled with shrimp, vegetables and fresh herbs. A true Vietnamese classic.

5

🍲 Seasonal Vietnamese Soup

A fragrant broth infused with local herbs and spices, made with the fresh produce from the market. Each season brings its own unique flavours.

6

🎁 Surprise Dessert!

We end on a sweet note with a surprise Vietnamese dessert... Come and discover it for yourself! 😉
